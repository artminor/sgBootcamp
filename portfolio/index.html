About Me

My name is Jun and I am fluent in three different dialects of Chinese in addition to English! Random fact aside, my coding journey technically started around October of 2017 when I finally decided to leave my job, focus on a career change, and search for a job that I would enjoy doing. I started an introductory class on javascript basics on Codecademy and soon realized I loved it! I transitioned from working for the Department of Education to become a Realtor so I can have more time learning to code. I tried to teach myself how to code through various websites. I have spent countless hours watching YouTube tutorials for the “easy-learning” component, and then I would practice the basics that I have learned on Codewars’ katas. After self-studying JavaScript on and off for a few months, I went from knowing zero lines of code to being offered to be part of Tech Hire Open Code Program’s Software Guild component. I am here and ready to learn. Now, I am officially another step closer to becoming a web-developer, and the best part is that it is free! Hopefully, I will make it into and through the program. When I graduate from the program, I will be equipped with enough skills to become a confident junior developer in the tech world. Hello World!
